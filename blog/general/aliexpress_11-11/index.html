11-11 (11 November) is Singles’ Day in China. According to Wikipedia, the date was chosen because the numeral 1 resembles a bare stick, which is Chinese Internet slang for an unmarried man who does not add ‘branches’ to the family tree. This Chinese unofficial holiday is also the introduction of the Chinese shopping season. That’s why, on sites like AliExpress and Banggood, you’ll find huge discounts on November 11-12.

To get the biggest discounts on AliExpress, make sure you add the items you want to buy to your shopping card during the Warm-Up period, which runs between October 28 00:00:00 and November 10 23:59:59 PST (UTC-7). Then enjoy the discounts buy actually buying the items between November 11 00:00:00 and November 12 23:59:59 PST (UTC-7).

So if you want to support me, this website and my projects, use any of the links I posted or the banner below before adding items to your shopping cart.
